About Us

AVSGHSS Karivellur, one of the renowned institutions in Kerala dazzles on the NH-17 at the heart of Karivellur. A readily accessible Higher Secondary School locates in the 6 acres of lushy greenery of Onakkunnu, inculcating the values and spirits of education to the public. Though the school was established in June 23, 1958 as a single teacher school with 32 students. Now it is at it's acme in all the fields of education with about one thousand students and sixty teachers.We dedicate these achievements on the selfless service and efforts of our beloved teachers, parents and Welfare committie members.
